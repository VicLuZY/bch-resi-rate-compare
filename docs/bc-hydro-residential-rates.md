# BC Hydro Residential Rates

Reference date: 2026-05-24.

The rates below list current BC Hydro residential electricity rates from official BC Hydro sources. Customer-facing rate pages list two base residential plans, tiered and flat, and optional time-of-day pricing that can be combined with either plan. The Electric Tariff prevails if there is any difference between summaries and tariff text.

All prices are before taxes and levies unless noted. Energy prices are in cents per kWh and fixed charges are in cents per day.

## Current Residential Base Plans

| Plan | Tariff schedule | Effective | Basic charge | Energy charge | Minimum charge | Notes |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|---|---|
| Residential Tiered Rate | 1101 | 2026-04-01 | 23.44 cents/day | Step 1: 11.87 cents/kWh; Step 2: 14.08 cents/kWh | Basic charge | Step 1 is the first 675 kWh/month or 1,350 kWh/two months, prorated daily. BC Hydro's customer page gives the daily threshold as 22.1918 kWh/day. |
| Residential Flat Rate | 1151 | 2026-04-01 | 25.00 cents/day | 12.70 cents/kWh | Basic charge | Available at the customer's election for residential service. |

## Optional Time-Of-Day Pricing

Time-of-day pricing is Rate Schedule 2101. It is not a standalone base rate; it is an optional adjustment used with Rate Schedule 1101 or 1151, including separately metered EV charging at a dwelling or premises that also receives service under 1101 or 1151.

| Period | Local time | Adjustment |
|---|---|---:|
| Overnight | 11 p.m. to 7 a.m. | -5.00 cents/kWh |
| Off-peak | 7 a.m. to 4 p.m.; 9 p.m. to 11 p.m. | 0.00 cents/kWh |
| On-peak | 4 p.m. to 9 p.m. | +5.00 cents/kWh |

Pre-rider energy prices after the time-of-day adjustment:

| Base plan component | Overnight | Off-peak | On-peak |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Tiered Step 1 | 6.87 cents/kWh | 11.87 cents/kWh | 16.87 cents/kWh |
| Tiered Step 2 | 9.08 cents/kWh | 14.08 cents/kWh | 19.08 cents/kWh |
| Flat | 7.70 cents/kWh | 12.70 cents/kWh | 17.70 cents/kWh |

Rate Schedule 2101 excludes the deferral account rate rider and trade income rate rider from the time-of-day adjustment itself.

## Current Rate Riders

As of 2026-04-01:

| Rider | Tariff schedule | Value | Applies to |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Deferral Account Rate Rider | 1901 | -1.5% | Applies to amounts otherwise payable under most tariff schedules before taxes and levies; excludes Rate Schedule 2101. |
| Trade Income Rate Rider | 1904 | 0.0% | Applies to amounts otherwise payable under most tariff schedules before taxes and levies; excludes Rate Schedule 2101. |

BC Hydro's residential rate page says the 2026 net bill increase is 3.75%, made up of a 0.59% annual rate increase and a rate rider amount change.

## Not Current Residential Options

As of 2025-04-01, BC Hydro says non-integrated area rates for Zone II and Zone IB areas, as well as multi-residential service rates, are no longer available for residential and business customers.
